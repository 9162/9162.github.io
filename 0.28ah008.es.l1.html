Title:
Exchange of prisoners in Viana (Navarre): 16th June 1875
Document number:
1729
Author:
Urgelles, L.
Printer:
Imprenta de Fidel Giro, Barcelona
Book/ Album / Dossier:
Estandarte Real, el : revista político-militar ilustrada
Format date:
1890
Topic date:
1875
Physical description:
1 26 x 34 cm engraving on a 31 x 41 cm page
Notes:
February 1890 edition. Autographed. It illustrates the article "Exchange of prisoners in Viana" by Flordelís next to a copy of the exchange agreement. "The recognition and adjustment of the lists began [...]. They began to call out the prisoners from a list, and they [...] moved to the side where they had to remain; the same number were then called out from the opposing side...".
