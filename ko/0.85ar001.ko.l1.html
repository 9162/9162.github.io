Introduction:
The Beijing Haidian Shangli Foreign Language School (SFLS)， a full-time international boarding school, was founded in 1993. As one of the best private schools in China, it has been approved and recognized by the Beijing Municipal Government. Our school has a 70,000 square-meter campus that is located just north of the beautiful Xian Shan Park and is only 5 kilometers from the center of Beijing.
At the Beijing Haidian Shangli Foreign Language School, we provide a nurturing and safe environment that emphasizes academic excellence from both our students and our teachers. Furthermore, we provide air conditioned classrooms, a computer lab, music and study rooms and excellent athletic facilities. Each dormitory includes an independent bathroom, air conditioning, refrigerator, fresh drinking water and caring dormitory management.
Our school includes an elementary, junior middle, high school and an international department. Over the years we have developed a course of study for international students who hope to be fluent in Mandarin. All of our teachers have had a rich teaching experience。

Principal gives a speech:
Welcome to Shangli Foreign Language School. We have created an environment in which you will make new friends, gain knowledge, and learn more about Chinese culture. We will help you to be successful in your education.

International exchange:
Over the past 13 years, the international department has trained many students from the US, Japan, Korea, Indonesia, Thailand, and more. Our school’s teachers and students all welcome you to Shangli Foreign Language School.

Course information:
Chinese Course:
The elementary and intermediate classes last for half or one whole year. The courses taught include Chinese speaking, reading, listening, writing, physical education, computing, and HSK　preparation．Professional　teaching materials and specialized teaching methods are used.

Chinese Diploma Course:
For students who have already passed HSK level 3, there is the opportunity to enter the Chinese students’ class. Foreign students who want to study at Beijing University must pass the HSK with at least a level 6. This exam includes the following areas of study, all of which are taught in the courses at SFLS: mathematics, foreign language, Chinese, and related curriculum. Foreign students will receive a diploma from SFLS after completion of these courses. And we can give foreign students all information about Chinese universities. Foreign students can use our diploma applied any Chinese university they wants to study. We do help foreign students choose suitable university and course, send recommendation letters to the university which they applied.


Ten major characteristics for Chinese teaching:
1. Clear teaching goals and advanced teaching methods. SFLS recruits foreign students to take the entrance exam to Beijing University, and uses this as the academic goal of the class.
2. SFLS has established a different level of study appropriate to each age and level of ability.
3. each class amount 20 students
4. Our teachers have had extensive and rich teaching experiences.
5. The style of teaching is lively, relaxed, and creates a cheerful environment for studying Chinese.
6. SFLS has created an environment conducive to language exchange between Chinese teachers and students with foreign students.
7. There are many possibilities for activities on weekends and vacations.
8. Foreign students may study other languages as well—not only Chinese.
9. Our courses for foreign students include teaching about traditional Chinese culture, helping students to understand Chinese culture, art, history, geography, society and more.
10. Foreign students receive frequent feedback from teachers, and an academic recorded is kept carefully throughout the semester

Life in school:
We look after every student, while letting each one arrange his or her own academic plan. Through careful instruction, students are taught responsibility and are given the skills needed to be able to manage themselves.

Board and lodging condition：
Boarding and lodging condition:
Foreign students’ dormitories consist of a shared bedroom with a private bathroom. Air conditioning, refrigerator, hot water, water dispensers, and a desk are provided. Health and basic medical care are available at the school clinic. Laundry, security, and phone services are also available.
