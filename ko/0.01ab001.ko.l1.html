We drink tea every day without a second thought. But in this story, the tea bags that we use on a daily basis contain something far more fantastic. Four students are waiting inside a classroom that is under construction. Each of them drinks a different kind of tea and imagines the stories that are contained within their tea. But the last student imagines something that should not be imagined.
 
 
After dinner one evening, I was about to put a tea bag into a steaming cup of water when I became suspicious of the contents of the tea. What exactly was hidden in that foggy bag? Was it just the ordinary dried leaves shown in the friendly illustrations on the packaging? I wondered if it might be something much spookier...
