Booking online






Hotel Carillon was founded in 1953 by Comm. Peppino Racchelli and has remained in the Racchelli family ever since for 3 past generations. Built directly on the lake shore it enjoyes stunning views of Lake Maggiore and the surroundings mountains.

In a few minutes walk along the pedestrian / cycle path you can reach Feriolo : typical fisherman’s village : quaint and picturesque. Stresa : the pearl of Lake Maggiore is only 3Km. away. From there or from Baveno you can board the boats offering frequent regular services to visit the Borromean Islands and indeed to reach all villages on the lake shore including Ascona and Locarno ( in the Swiss northern part of the lake ).

Feriolo is an ideal center for excursions to the lakes region ( Lake Orta, Lugano,Como). Milano is only one hour away either by car or by train ( railway station Stresa ). Torino can also be reached in one and a half hours.
