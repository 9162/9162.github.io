Inauguration at the Salto School (Uruguay)0

With the presence of the Bishop, Pablo Galimberti, the first stage of the “Brother Juan Bosco Correa” building in the Holy Family School in Salto was inaugurated. After the blessing and the new location of the image of the Blessed Mother in the new building, the primary school students went up to the classrooms to pray a prayer of thanksgiving.

The new building consists of a classroom and a large room for various school activities, as well as stairs and ramps leading to the school and a pastoral room.
