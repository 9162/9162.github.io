On the trail of ghosts and "tree beards"

Shrouded in legend and myth, the "Oberperfer Ghost Walk" through the forest to the reservoir, offers an adventure for the whole family.

The walk starts with a ride in the eight-seater cable-car that brings you up to Stiglreith at an elevation of 1,360 metres. This is the starting point of the themed walk that includes 21 intriguing stops along a one-and-a-half kilometre-long trail.

Accompanying you along the way are the "tree beards", each with their individually carved faces – and they can even talk! At the press of a button they tell you mysterious and wondrous stories. After all the suspense and exercise, most "little ghosts" are ready for a rest and a tasty treat, which can be had at Restaurant Sulzstich or the Stiglreith panorama restaurant.
