Description

Smart is your newborn, compact and ready to go inner city solution. With an intuitive compact 1 piece standing foot fold, and on 57cm in width, its perfect for those tight inner-city spaces. Smart is the most convenient, intuitive and clever little buggy you will find!

Easy foot fold
With a 1 piece foot fold that compact stands, as well as an adjustable handlebar and a light-to-touch handbrake, smart is the most convenient, intuitive and clever little stroller you will find.

Newborn ready convenience
Unlike others, smart is a complete newborn ready solution in full lie flat mode, as it has a lie-flat sling that can keep your precious newborn safe, calm cozy.

Superslim for tight inner city spaces
With its super slim-jim 22.4 inch width, smart folds for ease and convenience. Storage or space is never an issue in apartments, car trunks, or tight little cafes.

Super-smooth hassle free riding
A smooth ride for baby and parent! complete with 10 inch puncture proof aerotech tires, all wheel suspension and a fabric sling that puts weight in all the right places for the ultimate, superior curb pop, smart knows how to handle the city better than any other 4-wheel stroller!

Convenient hand operated parking brake
Convenience is important when you are on the go, and that is why the phil teds smart stroller has been created with the most easy peasy instinctive design out there. a convenient hand operated parking brake means you will have easy on/off control at your fingertips, to ensure a convenient ride for parents of all sizes.
