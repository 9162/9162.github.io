Description - Hotel

Grand Hotel is set in Fès, 1. 2 km from Royal Palace Fes. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Free WiFi is featured throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

Borj Fez Mall is 700 m from Grand Hotel. The nearest airport is Saïss Airport, 13 km from Grand Hotel.
