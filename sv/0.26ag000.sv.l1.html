Engines after year 2000 (engine codes in parentheses) have 19mm wrist pins as standard. Therefore these pistons only fit if you also change to conrods for 20mm pins.

Pistons for 19mm pins can be special made to order, please contact us in these cases. We however recommend an upgrade since these conrods are not suitable for increased power and rpm.
