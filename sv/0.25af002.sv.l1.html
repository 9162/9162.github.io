Included in SIReFAZ Advanced:
SIReUA1Z, control unit with built in room temperature sensor. Wall unit cover included.

SIReA1XN, PC board HUB Advanced

SIReOTX, outdoor temperature sensor

SIReCC, modular cables, RJ12 (6p/6c), 3 m resp. 5 m


SIRe can be set to 18 languages, divided into two product versions.

  • SIRexxxY communicates in Swedish, Norwegian, English, German, French, Spanish, Dutch, Italian, Danish and Finnish.
  • SIRexxxZ communicates in Russian, Ukrainian, Polish, Czech, Slovak, Romanian, Hungarian, Turkish, English and German.
