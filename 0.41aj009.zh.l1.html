Play the Israel Lottery

The Israeli Lottery is popular not only in Israel but also outside of the country.

Israeli lotto draws are held twice a week on Tuesdays and Saturdays 22:55.

The Jackpot may be subject to state tax of up to 25%.

To purchase one entry, players need to choose 6 main numbers each number ranging from 1 to 34. One additional bonus number also needs to be selected ranging from 1 to 10.
