What is a RM video?
Rights-managed video are high-quality products with a license and fee based on your specific use. Details regarding usage, media, duration and geographic distribution of use must be specified. The buyer may also choose to purchase an exclusive license, which prevents other clients for using the same video in the specified period.

What is a RF video?
Royalty-free pricing is based on the clips you need, not the specific use. You don't have to pay any additional fees on a use-by-use basis. Once you purchase a royalty-free product, you may use it multiple times for multiple projects without paying additional fees. Royalty-free videos offer creative professionals an alternative to right-managed video -- without sacrificing quality or creativity. Royalty-free products provide a solution for every budget, with few usage restrictions. Once you pay the fee for a royalty-free product, you can use the videos as frequently and creatively as you'd like.

How to quickly find a HD video of specific size?
You will find an "option column" on the left of the search result, and you can choose SD or HD and then the Hr size.

Search Tips
When running a search, if you need a video that does not have any people, you can input 'no people'. In general, if you don't want any particular object to appear, simply input 'not' before the key word. For example, if you would like an ocean video without any ship, you may input 'ocean not ship' to search.
