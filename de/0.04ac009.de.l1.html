The customer team of Hupac – intercultural and flexible

 

Thanks to the professional experience, the intercultural profiles and the dedication of all team members, we are ready to serve our customers even better. The team advises clients in the setup of traffics and takes care of all operational aspects. Our team of consultants is recognized for its outstanding expertise in combined transport, the flexibility and the longstanding professional experience.

 

Whether it is about a few consignments on single trains, the extensive utilization of the whole network or the commitment for slots or entire trains – we develop user-specific solutions for all transport requirements together with our customers.
