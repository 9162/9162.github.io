Charming Suite
Located in a small cosy and historical street in the heart of Gamla Stan (old town) and just 200 meters from the Royal Castle, this charming apartment is located in a house from the 15:th century. On the ground floor: the bedroom, kitchen and bathroom and down the stairs on a basement floor: the living room including a convertible sofa for 2 extra persons. Great Choice to be in the Old Town!
