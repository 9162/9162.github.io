Cross section of material

IboFelt is a further material for trim components. As a sandwich system this concept consists of two stabilizing layers of fibres which encase a lightweight, sound absorbing core. Basic materials are polyester and polypropylene fibres which we harden in varying degrees and densities to the corresponding layers.

If necessary, iboFelt can also be supplied with a layer impervious to sound and/or water in order to provide wheel arch liners, for example, with an additional insulation function.
