Last Section of Line 2 Lowered onto the Seafloor

In the early morning of April 18, 2012, pipe number 99,953, the last pipe of Line 2, was welded onto pipeline, which was lowered onto the seabed off the coast of Gotland, Sweden by the Castoro Sei laybarge. A total of 199,755 pipes were used to form both of the Nord Stream Pipelines.
