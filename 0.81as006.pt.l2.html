IARU-R1 HF Committee Newsletter

    
Sunday, December 08 2013 @ 12:38 PM WET
Contribuição de: CT1END
Visitas 522
The latest newsletter reports that IARU Region 2 has modified its 40m band plan to harmonize again with Region 1

IARU Region 2 first changed their 40m band plan to be similar to Region 1 at a meeting in 2007 but in 2008 Region 1 changed their band plan which in practice caused some problems for Region 1 operators wishing to operate with Digimodes stations in Region 2.
