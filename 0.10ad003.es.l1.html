Detailed Product Description

Product Introduction

CaCO3 filler masterbatch can be applied into packing films, bag products, sheet products, plate products, injection molding products and so on.

Main Constituents

LLDPE/PE/LDPE/HDPE, CaCO3, many kinds of modified additives, etc.

Technical indexes

 a. Appearance: smooth surface, regular granules with little impurity, It has better plasticizing property.

 b. physical and chemical properties

    Density 1.7±0.5g/m3

    volatile matter ≤2.0%

MFR / MFI: 3-15（g/10min）

Product applications

Filler masterbatch can be applied into packing films, bag products, sheet products, plate products, secondary processed extruded products, suction molding products, blown molding prodcuts (such as bottle), injection molding products and so on.

Using method

With additive amount of 10%-50% of this product, low cost but high quality plastic products can be produced in the regular processing of ordinary plastic films, plastic bags and other ordinary products, such as extruded products, injection products and blowing bottle products. Please note that processing temperature is a little higher at the time of welded sealing.

Storing conditions

Keep it in a clean aeration drying and cool environment. Packaging damage and exposure and rain are not allowed.

Expiration date: 1 year.
