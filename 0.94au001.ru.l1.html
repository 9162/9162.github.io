About us

Offered services

Handling, mowing, planting.

Homestead handling

Complex and diverse homestead handling: waste disposal, undergrowth clearing, lawn seeding, conversion of forest areas into parks, etc.

Lawn and meadow installation and maintenance

Mechanized lawn and meadow installation in large areas. Grass and meadow mowing by mulching or collecting it.

Sosnowsky Hogweed eradication

Mechanized Sosnowsky Hogweed eradication in spring and fall by deeply plowing the land. It is advised to repeat eradication several years in a row, depending on the age of Sosnowsky Hogweed.

Forest care

Forest clearing from scrubs, undergrowths and windfalls. Forest, which is difficult to get through, is converted into a beautiful park.

Shrub and bush clearing

Mechanized shrub and bush clearing in fields and by the forests using a shredder. Shrubs, bushes, tree stems, stumps and roots are shredded by penetrating up to 10 cm deep into the soil.

Abandoned land restoration

Abandoned land restoration, i.e., shrub clearing, plowing, leveling of land and sowing of lawns or meadows. Use of combined equipment allows us to perform plowing, leveling of land and fertilizer (seed) penetration at the same time, which saves both time and money.

Territorial and road sweeping

Mechanized cleaning-sweeping of open areas and roads.

Snow removal

Mechanized snow pushing from roads, parking lots and open areas, as well as snow removal from roofs and snow disposal.

Process

It is important for us that the service is as easy and convenient as possible.

  • 1. Communication

    You contact us by phone or e-mail.

  • 2. Need assessment

    We assess your needs.

  • 3. Proposal submission

    We submit terms, conditions and prices within 24 hours of your request.

  • 4. Execution of services

    According to our agreement we operatively and qualitatively perform our services.

  • 5. Payment

    You pay for the services, by credit card, bank transfer or cash.

Long term homestead services

At your request, we provide long term homestead handling services. All work is done in accordance with a pre-arranged schedule. We inform you about our arrival by an SMS message 24 hours in advance.
