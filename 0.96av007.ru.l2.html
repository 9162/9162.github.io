Airport transport will end

Airport transport from Jyväskylä city centre to the airport will end by the end of this year.

For the transport, a fee of €8 has been charged from passengers and the transport has been partially aided with public funds. The operator has been S&P Lehtonen.

Bus transport has been used to a rather small extent. By average, there were one to three passengers per shift on the route. Commercial grounds for continuing the transport do not exist, which means that the transport will end by the end of this year.
