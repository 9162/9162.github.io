How to rotate PSD? Nikon NIKON D5100

Svar

You can use our software Contenta Converter PREMIUM to rotate thousands of PSD. Download now

Step 1: Select your PSD photos

Click on the Add Photos button and select from your computer the PSD photos you wish to rotate. Then click the ‘Next’ button.

Step 2: Rotate your PSD photos

Go the Advanced tab and select Add Effect/Annotation->Composition->Rotate. You will be able to enter the angle of the rotation (in degrees). Click Start! and your PSD photos will soon be rotated.
