Establishing a Practice or a Prayer for a loved one

Pujas and prayers benefit beings in temporal and ultimate ways. They avert and clear obstacles to good health, longevity, success, happiness, contentment, peace and harmony, and also those that affect progress in spiritual realisation. Pujas and prayers dedicated to the dying pacify their minds and those offered to the deceased help support and guide the consciousness through their journey to rebirth.
