 .: Descripción: 
This composition for band is scored for bands of a high level. In this piece of music Rita Defoort and André Waignein highlight all characteristics of the wind band: character, feeling, quality and virtuosity. From the beginning spectacular music is being played with augmentations. The contiguous different pieces, based on perfect fourth intervals, are well connected, giving the piece a harmonious character. The orchestration is also flat-out sensational. After the introduction of the theme in the beginning of the piece, the oboe introduces a slow part. Then trumpets take over this movement, followed by the entire band. This part is followed by a fantastic percussion section solo, which introduces the final part of the composition. Many different composing techniques are applied in this piece of music: bitonality, polyrhythm, deliberate dissonants, glissandi, trills, etc All these aspects make this original work a true masterpiece.
