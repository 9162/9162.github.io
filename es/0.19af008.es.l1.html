Notes:
22nd of September 1887 edition. Autographed page (From life). To mark the visit by the Queen Regent and her children to Bilbao, they had made decorations such as the momumental arches "raised opposite the Zabalburu palace, it was slender and elegant, sumptuous yet simple at the same time, copied..., from the one that Moscow built in honour of Emperor Alexander III...".
