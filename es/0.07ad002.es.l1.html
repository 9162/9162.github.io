      LifePak Women Anti-Aging Formula

      LifePak Women provides a comprehensive formula of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants especially developed for pre – and perimenopausal women.

      Buy Now

      Product Details

      • Benefits
        • Supplies an optimum level of bone nutrients
        • Offers superior anti-aging benefits and cell protection against damaging free radical attacks by providing the body with important antioxidants and phytonutrients such as alpha-lipoic acid and catechins
        • Improves and supports your antioxidant defense network
        • Supplies a comprehensive blend of nutrients to support a healthy cardiovascular system
        • Promotes healthy immune function
        • Supports normal blood sugar metabolism
        • Corrects nutritional deficiencies
      • Usage
        Recommended adult use--Two packets daily: Take the contents of one (1) LifePak Women® packet with eight (8) ounces of liquid with your morning and evening meals. Each packet contains one (1) vitamin capsule, two (2) phytonutrient capsules, and three (3) mineral capsules.
        NOTE: Pregnant or lactating women, diabetics, hypoglycemics, and people with known medical conditions should consult with a physician prior to taking supplements.
        WARNING: Accidental overdose of iron-containing products is a leading cause of fatal poisoning in children under 6. Keep this product out of reach of children. In case of accidental overdose, call a doctor or poison center immediately

        Do not use if shrink wrap is damaged or missing, or if packet is broken. Store in a cool, dry place.
