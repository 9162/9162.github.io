Cerebral Palsy of North Jersey (CPNJ) is dedicated to enhancing the lives of people with disabilities and other special needs by supporting personal growth, independence and participation in the community.

New Jersey Coalition for the Advancement of Assistive and Rehab Technology – NJCART

Posted on December 20, 2011 by katherine

On December 7th, CPNJ’s AAC/AT Department hosted a meeting for NJCART. This meeting, open to the public, featured the Department’s own Julie Johnston and Melissa Tuleya as guest speakers for a presentation on the considerations for choosing switch type and placement and supporting switch users with free and low cost resources found on the Internet.

For more information on NJCART and their upcoming meetings, visit http://www.njcart.org. As always, keep checking this website for more professional development opportunities about Assistive Technology and Augmentative and Alternative Communication.

Football Tournament Set for June 30th

Posted on December 12, 2011 by david

CPNJ’s 27th annual Aubrey Lewis Football Classic will be held on Saturday, June 30, 2012 in MetLife Stadium. Teams from throughout the tri-state area compete in this 6-on-6 touch football tournament. Slots are expected to fill up quickly, so we encourage teams to register well in advance. For more information, email David Bishop at dbishop@cpnj.org.
