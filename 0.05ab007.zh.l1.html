What should I look out for when staying for long periods in a place with strong air-conditioning?

Those who are concerned with skin dryness should check the room temperature regularly and always keep the temperature slightly warmer.

People with sensitive skin should not expose their skin so as to prevent wind draughts from blowing directly onto skin. It may be necessary to put on a light cardigan or shawl even during sunny days.
