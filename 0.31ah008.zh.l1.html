Room and Accommodation

The suites feature 1, 2 or 3 bedrooms, accommodating 2, 4 or 6 people, respectively. They are all equipped with internet access, cable TV, telephone, safe and air conditioning. Guests bringing laptops can use free Wi-Fi access in the lobby .
