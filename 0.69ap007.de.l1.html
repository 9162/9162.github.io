Silicone – Replacement

As an alternative to low viscous silicones (Cyclomethicone, etc.) is Soldoc VF 8 (Neopentyl Glycol Diethylhexanoate) available for you. Are higher viscous silicones (Dimethicones, etc.) supposed to be used, we offer you Soldoc VF 4/18 (Pentaerythrityl Tetraisostearat).

Because of the different chemical structures of the silicones and the “replacements” it is not a substitution but rather should be seen as an alternative to achieve the sensory characteristics of silicones.
