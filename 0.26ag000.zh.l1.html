Picture Recovery on Mac

Digital cameras are popular worldwide for high quality pictures they produce using advanced image processing techniques. Some digital cameras are commonly known as DSLR cameras and are preferred by people all around the world as they produce better quality photos than simple digital cameras. Canon, Nikon, Sony, Samsung, Pentax, Kodak, Panasonic etc are few well known digital camera brands that uses separate RAW photo file format to store pictures.

However, due to some problems you might end up losing all important photos from digital camera. Picture loss could happen either while viewing pictures on camera or else while accessing photos on Mac system. In either of the case, you need to take help of third party Mac picture recovery tool to restore all lost/deleted pictures from memory card or from Mac volumes in different situations as mentioned below:

  • You might accidentally delete pictures from camera’s memory card by pressing “Delete All” button
  • While previewing pictures on digital camera, you might format the memory card by selecting “Format” option
  • Taking photos when camera is low on battery may even delete pictures
  • Improper plug-in or plug-out of memory card onto the Mac computer could lead to deletion of pictures on memory card
  • Abrupt shut down of Mac system during the transfer of photos from camera memory card to Mac system or vice versa could result in the loss of photos
  • Unintentional deletion of photos by using “Command + Delete” key combination or emptying deleted files from Trash can also delete pictures
  • Accidental formatting of Mac volume might even result in deletion of pictures
