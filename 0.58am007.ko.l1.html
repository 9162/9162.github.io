Tomatis® Certification

Professionals listed on this website have been trained by Tomatis Développement S.A. Company and have signed a License agreement. They are equipped with high-quality devices and have a deep knowledge of how to use them. But it is the client’s responsibility to be sure that the professional’s profile fits with his/her specific needs. Tomatis Développement S.A. is not involved in any way or manner in an individual client’s relation with the professional contacted.

Consultant

A Tomatis® Consultant (level 3 or 4) is a professional who has completed the training levels 1, 2, 3 (and 4) and has a deep understanding of how to administer and interpret the Listening Test and is therefore able to completely individualize and personalize the listening programs of his/her clients.

Practitioner (level 2)

A Tomatis® Practitioner (level 2) is a professional who has followed both the initial training and a complementary training (level 2), qualifying him/her to use the Tomatis® Listening Test System (TLTS) and the active voice stimulation device (TalksUp® level 2). The TLTS is necessary for determining a listening profile for each individual. The TalksUp® (level 2) is used to complete listening sessions by active audio-vocal sessions.

Practitioner (level 1)

A Tomatis® Practitioner (level 1) is a professional who has followed the initial training (level 1) allowing him/her to use the TalksUp® portable equipment. These devices reproduce faithfully the Tomatis® Effect and contain several listening programs. The professional is able to choose which of these programs best fits the client’s needs based on assessment tests. These programs address the most important listening issues and problems.
