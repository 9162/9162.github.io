Alexei Miller Speaking at We Deliver Event

On October 8, 2012, just 30 months after the start of construction, both lines of Nord Stream's twin pipeline system came on-stream. This occasion was celebrated at an event held in Portovaya Bay, Russia, near the starting point of the pipelines at the Baltic Coast. At the event, Alexei Miller, Deputy Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chairman of the Management Committee, OAO Gazprom explained that Gazprom took great efforts on developing the Russian gas transmission system that will securely deliver gas into the Nord Stream Pipelines. He explained that at full capacity, the Portovaya Compressor Station is the most powerful compressor station in the world. He also mentioned the expansion of Nord Stream. “Today Nord Stream shareholders considered a preliminary feasibility study for the third and fourth legs, their construction was recognised as economically expedient and technically possible.”
