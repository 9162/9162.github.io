The river beach Mato Village

The river beach Mato Village is located approximately 20 minutes, the Casa João Chagas. "The Mato Village River Beach was honored this year with the Blue Flag award, a symbol of environmental quality reference. In approving the nomination submitted by the city of Abrantes, the European jury and the National Commission of the European Blue Flag Association (ABAE) certified the environmental quality and recognized the effort of several entities, in order to improve the environment in the place where it is located. The European Blue Flag is an important award that distinguishes the riverbanks for its quality, safety and equipment. It is also synonymous with environmental quality internationally recognized and imposes strict conditions on water quality, environmental education and information, environmental management and equipment. Water quality is the main criteria for obtaining this symbol. In the district of Santarém, the only river beaches that will fly the Blue Flag are Mato Village (Abrantes) and Carvoeiro (Mação).
