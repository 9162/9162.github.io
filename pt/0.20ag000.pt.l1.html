MERCURY Apartment Zakopane Center 

Description
Opinions
Price

MERCURY apartment is located in a quiet area just a few minutes walk from the lively Krupówki Street.
Nearby you can take advantage of the wide range of catering, sport and cultural activities, which prepared the town of Zakopane.
Just 600 meters from the apartment there is a modern City Park with its many attractions such as a children's playground,
basketball court and clay courts for tennis. After an exhausting day full of excitement can relax in a modern interior,
which is a combination of gray, white and wood. Another advantage is a large balcony where you can enjoy views of the Giewont and Gubałówka savoring your morning coffee.

Apartment Mercury is located in the newly wykońcczonej villa that attracts its interesting finish.
Austerity and minimalism, krorą can be found in the middle with an unusual yet elegant composition.
In the middle you can relax families with children and groups of friends. The apartment is prepared for max. 4 people

 In The Mercury Suite you can find:

SALON - with a mini kitchenette with fridge, sink, kettle, cups, plates, cutlery, table with chairs, a sofa bed with coffee table, TV, Wi-Fi, access to a terrace with table and chairs balcony

BEDROOM - large comfortable double bed, bedside tables, lamps, wardrobe, exit to the terrace

BATHROOM - shower, sink, hair dryer, towels

Mercury apartment is located on ul. Orkana 8 b.
