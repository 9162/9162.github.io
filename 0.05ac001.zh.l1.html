Several Media Report the Interview of Congo-Brazzaville’s President to the International Students from Congo-Brazzaville Funded by CRBC

On June 18, Congo-Brazzaville’s President Denis Sassou-Nguesso gave an interview to the international students from Congo-Brazzaville fully funded by CRBC. Such important media as China Radio International “CRI Online” website and China Report made a follow-up report on this, and released news reports to publicize it.

On June 24, a news report titled President Sassou of Congo-Brazzaville Meets the Student of Congo-Brazzaville in China, Expecting Them to Build a Better Nation after Returning the Motherland was published on the website of China Radio International “CRI Online”. Link of the report:  http://gb.cri.cn/42071/2014/06/24/6071s4589045.htm

News page of “CRI Online” on June 24

On July 1, a news report titled Help the African Countries through Talent Cultivation —President Sassou of Congo-Brazzaville Meets the International Students of Congo-Brazzaville Fully Funded by CRBC was published on China Report.

“Enterprise” column of China Report in July, 2014

The above reports reflect the outstanding achievements in actively fulfilling the enterprise social responsibility CRBC made during the development and greatly enhance the brand image of the Company.
