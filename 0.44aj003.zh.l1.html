On the food production licensing system

Food production license of industrial products is an integral part of the permit system is to ensure food quality and safety, food production by the competent national quality supervision and administration department of the implementation of a food production and processing enterprises to control production conditions the control system. The system provides: engaged in food production and processing of citizens, legal persons or other organizations to ensure product quality and safety must have the basic production conditions, according to prescribed procedures were "food production license" before engaging in food production. Not winning the "food production license" business shall not produce food,
enterprises and individuals shall not sell any food without a license. 

Food safety market access system of meaning 

The so-called market access, generally refers to goods, services and capital levels of permission to enter the market. Market access for products, the general understanding is that the main market (the product manufacturer and distributors) and the object (product) level of permission to enter the market. So, food quality and safety market access system is, in order to ensure food quality and safety, the producers have the required conditions for production and business activities are allowed, provided the conditions have allowed food production and sales monitoring system. Therefore, the implementation of food quality and safety market access system is a government action, is an administrative permit system. 

Food quality and safety market access system, including three specific systems: 
 
(1) of the food production licensing system for production enterprises. To have basic production conditions, to guarantee food quality and safety of the enterprise, payment of "food production licenses" granted within the scope of production of the certified products; not get "food production licenses", the company allowed to produce food. This is from the production conditions to ensure the company can produce products that meet quality and safety requirements. (2) of the compulsory production of food inspection system. Without inspection or unqualified factory sales of food are not allowed. Do not have the self-test conditions for the production company to compel the implementation of commissioning tests. This requirement for Chinese enterprises existing production conditions and management level, effective safety always has the quality products manufactured. (3) on the implementation of the food production licensing system for the products from the market access labeling system. In certified food to print (paste) market access labeling --- QS mark, no sign affixed QS not allowed to enter the food market. Doing so, facilitate the identification and monitoring of consumers, the administrative law enforcement departments to facilitate supervision and inspection, also help to promote the production enterprises to improve their sense of responsibility for food quality and safety.
