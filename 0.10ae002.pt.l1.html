Chão Gonçalo Red


Type: Red

Region: Alentejo

Climate: Mediterranean

Soil: Argillaceous limestone and/or schist.

Method of cultivation (guide): Guyot Double Cord.

Average Production: 60 to 70 hl / ha

Grape Varieties: Lot -based Touriga Nacional with a touch of Petit Verdot.

Vinification: Fermentation at 21/22 ° C to observe the fruit and varietal character .

Analysis:
Alcohol content: 13,5% vol.;
Total acidity: 5.2 g /l;
pH: 3.6;


Tasting Notes:
Colour: Color Red.
Aroma: Varietal notes of Touriga with many flowers: Some violet, some bergamont and slightly peppered at the end.
Palate: Wine with elegant body, presents easy tannins and silky texture, acidity to the point and a medium ending. A young but well behaved wine at the table.

Preservation: Keep the bottle lying down in a cool, dry place.

Gastronomy: Ideal for meat dishes and baked fish , grilled meats and simple cheeses.

Temperature: Serve at a temperature of 14/16°.
