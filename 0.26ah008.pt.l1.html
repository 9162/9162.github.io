ADEGA MAYOR 9 (Nove) RED 2009

At 09:09:09 am on 09/09/09 9 people began filling the first 2009 bottles baptized with the universal number. A 49 years / light away, a team of American astronomers peering through the window cosmic, confirming to the world the discovery of a planet very similar to ours, the Super-Earth, with a radius three times larger than Earth, more than half covered water and its orbit around the sun itself.

Bottled in the International Year of Astronomy, the Wine 9 is a tribute to the number of heaven and humanity, the grandeur of the human being and its ability to transcend the universe to dream.
The clusters were vindimados manually and submitted to choice in the vineyard and in the cellar. The malolactic fermentation occurred in new French oak barrels, followed by an internship of 10 months. The plot of this wine, aged 15 months in bottle to reach the desired harmony.

9 Wine comes in a bottle with a map and a globe astronomical, totally handmade, glow in the dark. This third edition is limited.


GRAPE VARIETIES: Alicante Bouschet, Aragonez and Trincadeira

TASTING NOTES:
Aroma: A complex aroma characterised by intense notes of black fruits, graphite, chocolate, mint jelly, tabaco leaf, spices and coffee.
Palate: In the mouth it is full-bodied, thick and bold, endowed with excellent acidity and lively tannins, promising a long evolution in the bottle.

SERVING SUGGESTIONS: An imposing wine of notable quality characterised by its strong personality and perfect harmonisation with gastronomic delicacies with lots of condiment and an intense taste.
