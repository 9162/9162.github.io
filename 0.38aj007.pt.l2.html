-graphy

Significado de -graphy

pl.

A suffix denoting the art of writing or describing; also, the writing or description itself; a treatise; as, calligraphy, biography, geography.

Vogais: ay

Consoantes: grph

Palavras Parecidas

graph, grapy, grapey, geography, graip, grape, grapo, gravy, -grave, grabby.
