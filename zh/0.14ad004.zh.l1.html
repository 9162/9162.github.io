Vacuum filler DP14E

  • The fastest vacuum filler from VEMAG with rotary vane pump for industrial applications for all products
  • Filling rate of up to 10,700 kg an hour
  • Portion weights of 5 g up to 99,999 g
  • Portioning rate of up to 950 portions per minute
  • VEMAG Duo-Drive: two separate drives for conveying elements and feed

High flexibility
Extremely flexible thanks to the combination with VEMAG attachments for individual production.

Maximum weight precision
Gentle product conveyance and maximum weight precision.

VEMAG Box-in-Box System
The proven VEMAG box-in-box system offers perfect machine protection.
