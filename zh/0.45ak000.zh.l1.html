Research in China

The R&D Center is the first legal entity of KWS in China. And it is also the first R&D Center invested by a foreigner in Anhui Province. The business purpose is to create and establish a world-class, advanced technology new generation plant biotechnical research platform (According to the Articles of Association).

KWS Agriculture Science & Technology Research & Development (Anhui) Co ltd., (KWS R&D Center China Co.Ltd.) organized and existing under the laws of People's Republic of China, is a wholly foreign-owned R&D enterprise in China and having its principal place in Hefei, Anhui Province, represented by its Chairman, Dr. Haiquan Zhang. The company was registered in Anhui Provincial Administration for Industry & Commerce at 27 Nov 2009.
 
As approved in it's Business License, KWS R&D Center China is allowed to provides:
 
  • technical support and services (including product testing) in the fields of molecular biology, biochemistry and physiology, growth and biology, tissue cultivation and genetic transformation;
  • Research and testing of plant stress resistance and mineral use efficiency and other related fields;
  • related technology transfer and licenses within the above fields.
