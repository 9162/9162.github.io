公司

The Minelli leather goods company was established in 1972. It is based in Modena on an area of about 700 square metres and counts ten employees. Thanks to several external workshops, production is very flexible.

产品

The Minelli leather goods company manufactures women's handbags with a careful eye on fashion trends. Sales are made through the company's own sales network and exclusive agents. Our customers include major world large-scale distribution companies which appreciate the excellent value for money of our products. Current production touches around 40,000 pcs year with a turnover of ITL 1,600,000,000. Exports cover 50% of total turnover.
