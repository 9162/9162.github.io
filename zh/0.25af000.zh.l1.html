Hotel Introduction
It is the perfect hotel for business and leisure travellers. This Gothenburg hotel is minutes from the Gothenburg Stadsmuseum museum, the Gothenburg Opera , the Gothenburg Museum of Art and Ostindiefararen, an old freight ship.The top-notch conference facilities include 11 rooms for most events and business functions. Access to copy and fax services is offered. The hotel also has a currency exchange, car rental service and a picnic area for guest convenience. Laundry facilities are located on the property.All modern guest rooms have air conditioning, trouser presses, iron, ironing board and cable TV. In addition, some rooms have computer hook-ups. Handicap accessible rooms are available.
