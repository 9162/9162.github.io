呵呵/ /以上,았었/었었/였었,본 적 있다/없다 (to try, done before)

In this lesson, we will learn how to say how to try something, and to say whether we’ve done something before. AV + 呵呵/ / F + 보다 1. There isn’t really a verb for ‘to try’ in Korean, instead we conjugate the verb that we are trying to do into present form (呵呵/ / F) and append 보다 […]
