We found three things throughout the country and abundantly. Industry, much of it foreign, especially multinational, installed without a problem in the country, the conditions are advantageous and derisory prices of production. No matter how much reach to contaminate, tienen vía libre para hacer lo que quieran.


Pollution is a common problem in fast-growing countries, the same citizens pull everything everywhere, as if there were no tomorrow, as if like it somehow the small dump with rats in each corner. Obviously they would like to live in paradise, as almost everyone, but lack organization, money, conciencia social y vertederos oficiales.

 


No doubt they enjoy much more than food, they eat with their hands, without cutlery, playing and mixing thoroughly all the food on the plate. Clean-up is the hands after eating in the curtains of the bus, on scarf,… No matter, even in Bengali films they preach with example. The truth is that it becomes fun to see the world upside down, but to a certain extent. They have different criteria to our what is really clean or dirty, among other things, but I don't want to extend. The population is not concerned with ecology, esto forma un caldo de cultivo perfecto para que las chimeneas sigan escupiendo humo al exterior.

 


For some strange reason and as I have said many Bengalis, the fried chicken with hot sauce to rage is part of the cuisine of the country culture for many years. This famous bengali recipe should add any type of frying as e.g. samosas. Fried chicken can be found anywhere, crowd restaurants one after the other selling the same; serve it with rice or cucumber, que también les encanta y lo venden como aperitivo en cualquier rincón.

 


Another aspect that caught my attention is the extreme curiosity of the Bengali people; many of them you stare at all times and you review top down, as a movie star, Sometimes it becomes tedious to walk because all are over, greeting, asking to take pictures, Exchange a few words or wanting to sell something… you have to have patience and know that you have hurry, If you stop to say hello to all who ask for it you are not leaving the same street in hours, destacan por su extrema amabilidad y hospitalidad.
Among them, they discuss many times and don't hide, like scream when they do, attention. It seems that they will kill, but after a heated discussion they are reconciled, como bebés de teta.

 


Bangladesh has one of the largest Muslim populations in the world, with the permission of Indonesia. Es tanto el fervor religioso que a menudo los coches y autobuses se detienen en la mezquita más próxima a la hora del rezo.

 


Worst luck run women, especially Muslim women. They have a huge list of "things that they cannot do" or rules to follow as for example dress properly, no salir de casa sin compañía de alguien del sexo masculino y otras tantas que no voy mencionar.

 


I am going to relate a case which unfortunately I could see while traveling by bus. I could see how a Muslim young man responds to a couple their attitude, they were asleep, sitting beside her, with the head supported each other, I have no doubt a moment awaken them to correct its action. It was also often to speak or see how a friend slept, also Muslim and long beards that had just know, se le caía la baba solo de hablar con él.

 


Transport is crazy, I think that I never before spent so much fear traveling. Thousands of people die a year inside the bus, that is why I recommend using alternative such as boat or train transport, much slower but safer. I have suffered in my flesh overtaking of off-road double-decker buses, getting into mud roads to 80 per hour, regardless of anything the passers-by that there could be. The only rule of driving is : how much larger is the vehicle more preference it has and this rule is not always fulfilled. Lo mejor que pude hacer en estos casos es no mirar lo que hacen los conductores e intentar quitarme de encima las cucarachas que infestan la mayoría de autocares.
The State of vehicles is poor, most have huge holes and lack of parts essential for driving, oxide product, it rots the metal. In fact almost lost the suitcase, I was in the subject trunk of a single thread when we arrived at destination, There was a gaping hole in the roof, llegó de milagro.

 


Despite the sorry state of the vehicles in Bangladesh there is an enviable appearance, the extensive fleet of electric transport or gas. The thousands of rickshaws that circulate, especially in cities, they are electric and motor vehicles, I mean small taxis or CNG, all work with compressed gas, There are hundreds of gas stations of liquefied gas. En este aspecto están mucho más avanzados que otros países.

 


Another aspect to keep in mind is that there are people asking for money, even some transsexuals, It is customary to give them some money, they are usually very friendly and safe will make you complicit in a joke. Levels of begging are not comparable to the damage the India, where I've seen to cry more than one visitor tired of that the world is over, in Bangladesh it is tolerable and sometimes even fun if you take it with humor. In fact will you paste all the beggars, a few less funny than others, they will ignore the rest of the inhabitants to focus on tourists, “a business in the short term” , their level of insistence sometimes hates. If you don't want to give money at the time it is best pouting, say a resounding NO and ignore them, enseguida os dejarán en paz.


Bangladesh is a magical place of truth, still to be discovered, with wonderful people, sure that it enjoy !

The Jomuna River in the dry season, When flow drops, form sand banks where the poorest of the country going to live, es tierra de nadie.
The few possessions that have van transferring them as the water flow is increased, en la época de lluvias.


About 1 million people living on the banks of sand, they have no other place to go in a crowded country, they have a piece of temporary land, en medio del río.
To learn how to live go to the nearest town, Sariakandi and from there to ask. En época seca es posible llegar andando y en época de lluvias en barco.




His life is very fragile, Imagine living isolated in a wilderness, without trees to take shelter from the Sun, ni transporte público ni servicios de ningún tipo.




There are small stretches of sand with vegetation on the banks of the sandbanks, que es rápidamente recolectada o trabajada antes que llegue la época de lluvias y quede inundada.





Se sienten muy orgulloso de sus animales y pueden estar posando para la cámara todo el tiempo que sea necesario con tal conseguir un buen retrato.




Your diet is balanced, What causes some of its inhabitants, especially the younger, presentan pequeñas manchas en la piel como consecuencia de la falta de alguna vitamina.

Many adjectives can be associated with this city, and almost all unpleasant; starting from pollution, the disorder, dirt… I had the feeling of being enclosed within a large bucket of shit. If you visit Bangladesh to try to escape from this black hole as quickly as possible, but there is another option, stay in any of the districts for expatriates, businessmen and politicians such as Gulshan, Banani… donde se puede descansar parcialmente de esta caótica ciudad.


Another negative aspect of the city is poverty, omnipresent throughout the city, especially along the train tracks, where people trying to survive in the most dignified way possible. Fabrican sus casas con desechos que encuentran y hacen su vida a pié de vía.


If you are interested in learning to live without only resources you have to approach Tejgaon train station and take a stroll through the surrounding, is very easy to reach. It is a secondary train station, situada en el centro de la ciudad.


People make life between tracks and the children play almost always supervised by an adult, that alert the train pass, each 15 o 20 minutos aproximadamente.
The sad thing is that there are people worse living, como los habitantes de los bancos de arena que se forman en el río Jomuna.


The slums that crowd one by one have a very small space, the right to sleep, they cook and do their chores to free standing via. They are built of waste and plastics that can get. En esta atípica visita también se puede observar cómo se reciclan cartones o cómo golpean bombillas o desechos electrónicos para separar el metal.


If you want to know what is an overcrowded city you're in the right place, It is impossible to stroll quiet in the old part of the city, in your heart, Old dhaka, and in the majority of its bazaars, as the New Market, Banga Bazaar or Shankharia Bazar, located in the hindu. You always have someone in any direction seeking passage, pushing or touching the Bell for the bike, You can imagine jams forming there. Es una experiencia única que no os podéis perder y de la cual estaréis agradecidos de no vivir a diario.


It is a wild city where people fight as you can to move forward, but honestly, not steal or commit a crime. The Bengals will try to help you with what you need at any time and without hesitation. This in my opinion is one of the main attractions of the country, el factor humano.


If still not lost the nerves with the plans described above, bizarre activities like you and you like the idea of navigating through a rotten River ( certainly Bangladesh occupies the highest climax in my list of "travel" ) You can rent a boat of wood in the Sadarghat port, the same place where the famous Steamboat sails with blades called Rocket. You have two options, take a tour around the different ports of the river Buriganga and amazed of all activities that are carried out or you can simply cross the river and watch as they try to survive the citizens of the other shore cleaning or repairing corroded oil, ésta es una buena alternativa a los que desean ver la famosa chatarrería de barcos en la ciudad de Chittagong.

Not a single postcard in all Bangladesh, and not me strange, is a country without tourism, los extranjeros vienen por trabajo y se concentran en la capital Dacca.



The country became independent of the India in 1947 as East Pakistan and later in 1971 returned to independence, This time from Pakistan. Every year is celebrated the independence day, A 26 March, with many activities, especially in urban areas. Held the 26 March of every year, because so declared it their Sheikh and President Sheikh Mujibur Rahman in 1971, Although not got it up to the 16 in December of the same year, al derrotar las fuerzas Indo Pakistaní .


We cover the towns and streets, one after another, without a single tourist in sight and I wonder if I'll be a freak. Each town house has its puddle where clean, se asean y duchan.


We are like movie stars, even in the capital. Help us at all times and know where we came from, or simply say hello interested, without asking anything in return. During the trip I was interviewed 3 canales de televisión distintos.


Haggling is always present, but the majority of people are honest and not inflate prices, the change of the purchase is almost always correct, except for those working in the sector transport, que intentarán cuadruplicar el precio de cualquier manera.


It is one of the countries with the highest density of population of the world, approximately 160 millions of people on a surface 3 times smaller than Spain. It is impossible that we do not observe at all times, they are very curious, stay of standing in front of tourists, a circle, a Roman circus that is created spontaneously when a tourist is stopped a few minutes, as if they saw a Penguin in the desert. Algunos se quedan con la boca abierta y se les cae el cigarro.


The only tourists who visit the country are mostly businessmen and some tour scheduled in the places of greatest interest, such as the Sundarbans or Srimangal tea plantations, where you can also visit some tribal communities such as Adivasis, kashia, types, Tipra, Manipur…


Currently there are no postcards for sale in Bangladesh, but their 125 kilómetros de playa virgen todavía por explotar y el rápido crecimiento puede derivar en breve cientos de complejos turísticos.
