Hotel Introduction

This business apartment hotel is situated northwest of Rennes, in the Beauregard district. The northern ring road is easily accessible from the hotel, making it the ideal base for those wishing to explore both the town centre and the surrounding area. The Thabor Gardens, Saint Pierre Cathedral and the Rennes Opera are all located just 4 km from the accommodation, and Saint Jacques Airport and Nantes Airport lie approximately 9 km and 118 km away, respectively.

The studio (T1) apartments sleep 1 to 2 people and feature a kitchenette with a microwave, refrigerator, crockery and a dishwasher. The living rooms come furnished with a double bed, table, chairs, a desk, a TV and a direct dial telephone, and the en suite bathrooms have been fitted with a shower and toilet. All other apartments come equipped with a double bed, an ironing set, a direct dial telephone, TV, Internet access, individually regulated heating and a private balcony or terrace. The kitchens come with a refrigerator, cooker, microwave and washing machine, and the en suite bathrooms have been fitted with a shower, bath and hairdryer. A continental breakfast is served daily.
