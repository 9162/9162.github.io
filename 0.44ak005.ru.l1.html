Karaganda robot interested visitors of AMM-2017 exhibition (VIDEO)

Exclusive
A robot of "KAZPROMAVTOMATIKA" company interested many visitors of «Astana Mining & Metallurgy» exhibition - AMM-2017. About what this robot is and what it was created for, was said by Technical Director of "KAZPROMAVTOMATIKA" Dmitry Lepekhov in an interview with IA Strategy2050.kz.
Astana city 26 May, 16:31

KAZPROMAVTOMATIKA takes leading positions among domestic organizations working in the field of automation of technological processes due to the high engineering and technical potential of its qualified specialists and a well-developed partner network of developers and suppliers of automation equipment for the best world manufacturers.

"Our company is the only distributor in Kazakhstan of Mitsubishi Electric Factory Automation, which is an international giant in the field of production of electrical and electronic equipment. The company received a state license for the design, installation, repair of mining, metallurgical, explosion-proof electrical equipment, lifting structures," the Technical Director explained.

D. Lepekhov told about "Mitsubishi" robot, presented at AMM-2017, which interested many visitors of the forum.

"This is an industrial robot "Mitsubishi ". This stand was provided by the training center of the Karaganda State Technical University, which is called Mitsubishi Kazprom avtomatika KarSTU. Our students operate this equipment there and get acquainted with it at the learning stage and learn how to program it, understand its internal structure, and get the skills of its introduction into the technology. The robot is universal, designed for automation and mechanization of manual labor. And its speed presupposes that quality, the amount of operation that it can perform per unit of time, completely replaces the person. He replaces it in terms of accuracy, the number of operations, which are significantly larger than the person," D. Lepekhov said.

As he noted, the task of the company, which deals with a series of automation, assumes that those operations that are performed in the production with the help of manual labor, they will be adapted for the operations that the robot will perform.

"This robot can move at speeds of up to 10 meters per minute, positioned with an accuracy of 1 hundredth of a millimeter, it has built-in controllers that optimize and suppress various vibrations that occur in such fast modes. Robots are different, they are like this one with a hand, five-axis, so there are also simpler, more complex ones. These robots can be used in almost all industries. Since automotive, where they are most often used for automation, for example, welding bodies, and ending with various laboratory analyzes, for example, in the field of medicine," the Technical Director said.

He also noted that the company "KAZPROMAVTOMATIKA" develops new technologies in the field of robotics not for profit, but for the promotion and development of Kazakhstan’s robots.

"At the moment there are not so many robot manufacturers that really do it themselves. Our engineering company is 100% Kazakh, we are considering the automation of our industry, we are considering how to implement, adapt the robotics in production, and what economic effect it will result from, our task is not to make money to produce robots, but to produce the necessary Kazakh products with the help of a modern elemental, component base with modern software and a modern approach in this field," he concluded.

Recall, Astana hosts the VIII International Mining and Metallurgical Congress and the exhibition "Astana Mining & Metallurgy" - AMM-2017.

Kairat Zhandybayev
