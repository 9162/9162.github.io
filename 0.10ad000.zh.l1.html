SAMPE 2013

2013.05.06

May 06-09, 2013

Long Beach, California, US

The Society for the Advancement of Material and Process Engineering (SAMPE®) focus on advanced technology topics including composites, metals, ceramics, fabrication processes, polymers, reinforcements, adhesives, testing and analysis, and environmental issues. A strong emphasis on the market aspects of this technology provides an insight into current and future applications.

This annual event attracts over 5,000 international attendees encompassing all areas of advanced materials and processes. Growing each year, features include an unparalleled conference program boasting 10 concurrent tracks and over 300 presentations on the latest applications and market developments.

Find new answers to your project challenges in the exhibition hall, where 400 companies showcase products and that are redefining the industry.

Alantum will participate with its own booth Nr. S 50 and proudly present their alloy metal foam technology.

Come visit us at the booth and have a talk with our material experts and the management about future solutions for advanced technologies. To arrange a meeting during the conference, please send us an email to special-info@alantum.com

Go to the exhibition site: www.sampe.org
