A seminar was recently organized in the «Mouratsan» University Hospital of YSMU. It was aimed at the International Nurse Day celebration. Among the people present during the event were Armen Minasyan, YSMU Vice Rector for Clinical Affairs, Gagik Miridjanyan, the YSMU Head of Staff, and Bagrat Baveyan, Chief Doctor of «Mouratsan» University Hospital.

Civic Education. Children's Point of View

Нет перевода
15.05.2014

On May 13 the presentation of the «Explanatory Dictionary of Civic Education» took place in Herats high school of the Yerevan State Medical Univesity after M. Heratsi. The event was attended by Armen Ashotyan, the RA Minister of Education and Culture, Mikayel Narimanyan, the YSMU Rector, Vardan Poghosyan, Head of the Project on Legal and Judicial Reform in the South Caucasus, the authors of the dictionary, people responsible for education field.

First Joint Conference of American Union of Clinical Oncology (ASCO) and Armenian Association of Hematology and Oncology Organized in YSMU

Нет перевода
13.05.2014

The first joint conference of the American Union of Clinical Oncology (ASCO) and the Armenian Association of Hematology and Oncology was opened today in the Yerevan State Medical University after M. Heratsi, which was dedicated to the application of newest methods of treatment of breast cancer, cancer of the gastrointestinal tract and leukemia.
