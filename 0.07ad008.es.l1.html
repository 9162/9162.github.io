Whereas its name, Carattere Toscano was adopted quite recently, this group of Hotels e Resorts has been operating for many years, selecting and renovating hotels in the most characteristic and charming parts of Tuscany.

The itinerary traced out by the Group’s Tuscan hotels is a fascinating way to discover the true heart and soul of this magical region.

The many charming and authentic hidden corners and unique landscapes will remain as precious memories, from historical palaces in the heart of Florence and Grosseto,  to the splendid resorts along the coast  and set in the pristine nature of Castiglione della Pescaia as well as the many charming little towns in the heart of the Chianti region.
