Oris Big Crown Original Pointer Date

Back to the future Initially developed in 1938, our pointer date was ahead of its time – and so is this watch, with its oversized crown, stainless-steel case, applied fluted bezel, and skeleton case back. There’s a sense of history to this watch – and the spirit of classic aviation. The legend is back.

Case Oris Big Crown, 28.50 mm, 1.122 inches, Stainless steel
Material Multi-piece stainless steel case
Size 28.50 mm, 1.122 inches
Top Glass Plexi, domed
Case Back Stainless steel, screwed, see-through mineral glass
Operating Devices Stainless steel crown
Water Resistance 5 bar
Interhorn Width 14 mm
Movement Automatic winding pointer date
Developed by Oris.
Number Oris 584, base ETA 2688/2671
Dimensions Ø 19.40 mm, 8 3/4’’’
Functions Centre hands for hours, minutes and seconds, date by centre hand, instantaneous date, date corrector, fine timing device
