    DESCRIPTION

    Two-bedroom apartment at 9 floor, repair made in 2008, 2 separate bedrooms, living room-kitchen Studio.
    The apartment is situated in front of the shopping center "Mandarin Plaza" and "Arena City" is at your disposal 24-hour supermarket "Furshet" entertainment complex "Arena" (a night club, a casino, a sports bar, etc.), luxury boutiques, perfume shops "Brocard", restaurants and cafes. Modern art lovers can visit the exhibition at Pinchuk ArtCentre. Also you will find the Bessarabian Market, which is open 24 hours a day.
    Details: beautiful view from the window at the Bessarabskaya Square. Kitchen - compact built-in kitchen furniture, cooker with gas hob, a fridge, an electric kettle, dining table with chairs, kitchen utensils, washing machine. In the hallway there is a mirror, armored door. A bathroom with good sanitary ware, mirror, bathroom, glass shelves, all the necessary amenities. The apartment is air conditioned.
    Additional services - wireless Internet, iron, Ironing Board, hairdryer, vacuum cleaner.
