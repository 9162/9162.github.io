GRAND BOUTIQUE HOTEL SERGIJO**** Piešťany, Slovakia

Luxury hotel
Member of Small Elegant Hotels of the World

Grand Boutique Hotel Sergijo ****, a luxury Hotel in Piestany, located in the recreation area of Sĺňava II. It is the only international hotel in the area, located in the famous spa town of Piestany, only 80 km from Bratislava and 120 km from Vienna in direct motorway connection.

Location

Grand Boutique Hotel Sergijo**** is the most beautiful hotel around the spa town Piestany. History of the spa is closely linked with the history of the city since 1549. Emperor Leopold I. took over Piestany and surrounding spas under his protection in 1682. The principal sources of natural spa healing is in the gypsum-sulfur thermal water with a temperature of 67-69 C degrees (applied in pool and baths) and healing sulphurous mud (applied in the form of partial or total wrap). Spas are focused on the treatment of locomotor system diseases and neurological diseases. The spas are well known throughout the world because of its kind.

Grand Boutique Hotel Sergijo**** is located near the spa center and guests can benefit from an ambulant bath treatment. Piestany is the city with the most sunny days per year and excellent motorway connection with all major cities. Charter flights are available by the airport Piestany.

Description

Accommodation area:

Grand Boutique Hotel Sergijo**** has a capacity of 25 rooms, 2 VIP suites, 16 deluxe double rooms, 6 double bed rooms with extra double bed and 2 single bed room. The total accommodation capacity is 48 beds and 8 extra beds, total of 56 persons.

Rooms are equipped with a comprehensive luxury Italian furniture and room size is 28-45 m2 with balcony, complex equipment: King Size double bed with organic mattresses, writing table, chairs resp. sofa, minibar, safe, bathroom with Wellness showers by Hans Grohe and many exclusive accessories like lighting and paintings. All rooms are with balconies with the view on the lake, TV with Russia, France, Spain, Italy, England, Germany and many other international channels. All rooms have free fast WIFI internet connection.

Restaurant area:

LA REINE Restaurant
the only of its kind in the city of Piestany. Provides dining for 65 people with the possibility of arranging events and weddings up to 80 persons. Has excellent menu focusing on steaks and international specialties.

Cafe LA BELLA
offers nicely decorated sitting in an Italian style with a wide selection of coffees, home-made desserts, cocktails and tapas. Capacityis 20 persons.

English Lobby and Cigar Bar
exclusive bar in Old English style with pool table and seating for 20 people. Wide range of an exclusive cognacs, Cuban cigars and drinks. There is also a karaoke, Chess table or minirulette. In competition “Bar AWARD 2008” the lobby bar won 1st place as the best hotel lobby bar in Slovakia. (the judges were from Paris and England).

Exclusive wine cellar
with wines from French actors Gerard Depardieau and Pierre Richard: only one of its kind in Slovakia. We offer a wine tasting from French collection from actors in comfortable seating in a stylish wine room for 16 people.

Garden Grill Terrace
of our Hotel offer sitting from April to October on the terrace by the fountain with lots of flowers and offering grilled specialties, light salads, spring and summer of dishes, fresh pizzas, with a view of the playground with gazebo. Capacity for 65 people.

Summer terrace
of the Hotel is in chill out zone with whirlpool and water bed, with the possibility of relaxing in the garden. Excellent place for luxury summer weddings, up to 80 people in gazebo, suitable as well as a garden party.

Wellness and beauty center:

Grand Hotel Sergijo provides an excellent opportunity for relaxation in wellness and beauty center of the hotel. The Wellness Center offers a very wide range of professional and therapeutic massages, shiatsu, hot stone massage, Chinese and Thai massages as well a lot of wraps from the mud and chocolate, Israeli and etc. Beauty center has a wide range of cosmetic and beauty treatments for women and business treatment for men. Hotel has a manicure, pedicure, hairdressing services.

We offer a Finnish sauna for 12 people,jacuzzi for the ultimate relaxation. Next to the hotel is summer pool and the possibility of health bath in the nearby spa with therapeutic thermal water in Napoleon Spa.

Business lounges and business service

The hotel has 2 business lounges: BUSINESS meeting room for 50 people and a DIPLOMAT lounge for 20 people. Both rooms are equipped with: projectors, flipcharts, overhead projector, WIFI internet, large menu with coffee breaks and business menu. We offer comprehensive business service.

Sport and surroundings

The hotel offers a beautiful large garden in the walled center with fitness, sports facilities in the area: in front of the largest Slovak water-ski resort Ratnovská bays, cycling and hiking orbit around the lake SLNAVA and river Vah, tennis courts, golf course, the possibility of organizing hunting and fishing.

There are a number of chateaus and manor tours for the guests with the possibility of guided tours and transfers to Bratislava, Vienna, Budapest, High or Low Tatras or Piestany.
