IIBA certification and Babok V3

 

The IIBA has released the new model of certifications in Business Analysis: 4 levels of certificazione, from BA super junior to BA super senior, to bridge the needs of enterprises and professionals.

The new model will be not only specific for Business Analysts, but also it will encompass the enlarged community of Business Analysis practitioners... let's think for instance on the different perspectives described in the Babok V3, and not only to these.

The new model will be multi-disciplinary, multi-industry, and competency-based... in other words a true "revolution" in the Business Analysis world... we should expect great things from this "Global Standard" that will place the IIBA in a "tought leader" position, which is what the community of BA is expecting...

We remind those interested in attending the IIBA certification training to verify that the vendor:
1. is a company certified Endorsed Education Provider (EEP), only EEP's can provide training for the IIBA certifications
2. provides each student with a paper copy of the Babok Guide V3.
